patient_id            : Unique identifier for each patient
age                   : Age of patient in years
gender                : Reported gender (inconsistent formatting)
diagnosis             : Primary diagnosis during admission
length_of_stay        : Total days admitted in hospital
systolic_bp           : Systolic blood pressure (mmHg)
cholesterol           : Cholesterol level (mg/dL)
previous_admissions   : Number of past hospital admissions
readmitted            : Whether patient was readmitted (Yes/No)
admission_date        : Date of hospital admission
